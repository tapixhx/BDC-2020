About BDC 2020

Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College, in keeping with its policy of commitment towards society and to sensitize the students accordingly, is organizing its annual Blood Donation Camp in collaboration with Rotary Club & Vardaan Multispecialty Hospital Blood Bank.

"करके देखो अच्छा लगता है"

Social Awareness

Without pain there is no gain. It becomes exemplary when blood donation comes into spotlight, your little effort could give a gift of life to someone else. According to a WHO report, out of the needed 12 million units of blood only 9 million units are available.

Your 1 pint blood can be a life saviour for that accident victim or that cancer patient.

Moreover, blood donation safety will be ensured by AKGEC, itself. All the vials and syringes will be safe to use. Everyone will follow a proper code of conduct to ensure your safety.

Myths

Giving blood hurts and is time consuming.

Blood donation isn't for women.

Health deteriorates after donating blood.

A blood donor is prone to infection.

There is limited blood in the body and
it is unhealthy to give some away.

Facts

The gift of blood is the gift of life. There is no substitute for human blood.

A person can donate blood every 90 days (3 months).

One blood donation can save the lives of up to three people.

The blood type most often requested by hospitals is type O.
